About Ronnel

Ronnel Cruz is a fourth year student at the City College of New York pursuing a double degree in Marketing Psychology and Digital Design. His powerful stance on the technical and decorative aspect of the design industry have been harvested from his experiences in the past few years. Taking diverse courses designed to research the blueprints of consumer behavior and to understand why something exists for the reason it does. Current and previous fellowships with renowned companies such as General Assembly, Braven, Starbucks and Capstone Management have shaped him to be the leader that he is. Ronnel focuses his graphic and photographic work with an editorial point of view; fashion and human sexuality both present a visible consistensy on the majority of his work.
